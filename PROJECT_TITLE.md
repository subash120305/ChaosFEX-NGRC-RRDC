## Title Components Explained

### **ChaosFEX** (Chaos-Based Feature Extraction)
- Utilizes chaotic dynamical systems (GLS and Logistic maps)
- Extracts four key features: MFT, MFR, ME, MEnt
- Captures nonlinear dynamics that traditional CNNs miss

### **NGRC** (Next Generation Reservoir Computing)
- Simplified reservoir computing architecture
- Nonlinear vector autoregression
- Efficient temporal dynamics processing

### **Rare Retinal Disease Classification**
- 49 diseases from RFMiD 2.0 dataset
- Multi-label classification
- Focus on underdiagnosed conditions
